I T E C H R O O T S 12.O

ITECHROOTS is the technical festival of the department of Information Technology,YCCE Nagpur in association with Computer Society of India.We have been organizing techfest since 2009. Many events are been taken in account here at ITECHROOTS.
The ITECHROOTS 12.0 aims to provide platform to the students to exhibit their talent in various technical and non-technical events. ITECHROOTS 11.0 has witnessed participation of students from various engineering and other colleges throughout India. Get ready for astounding events!
